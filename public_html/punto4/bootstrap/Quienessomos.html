About us

26 de Junio de 2017

We are an Agroindustrial company dedicated to the production and supply, based on optimal quality controls, international standards and the preservation of the environment; With the aim of offering products of export to the global market with punctuality of delivery and consolidating good commercial relations based on the constant growth of our production and the satisfaction of our clients.

Mission

26 de Junio de 2017

Generate, innovate, apply and transfer technologies with the purpose of linking and consolidating the production-consumption chain of the agricultural sector as well as formulating projects that contribute to the development of agribusiness to meet the needs of the same at national and international level.

Vision

26 de Junio de 2017

In 2020 we will be a leading company in agroindustrial production, based on standards for quality in production, and having as reference the international market to grow and respond to the needs of the agronomic sector
